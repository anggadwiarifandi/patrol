title: Get in Touch with the Patrol Team
---

We're here to help you get the most out of Patrol. Whether you've run into an issue, have a question, or just want to share your thoughts, you're in the right place. Choose the option that fits your needs best:
- <span style="font-size: 18px;">**Found a bug?**</span> [Create an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/leancodepl/patrol/issues/new) so we can review it.
- <span style="font-size: 18px;">**Not sure how to use something, or it's not working?**</span> [Join our Discord community](https://discord.gg/ukBK5t4EZg) and ask your question in the dedicated channel.
- <span style="font-size: 18px;">**Want to share feedback?**</span> Let us know what you think through [our feedback survey](https://forms.gle/example-feedback-form).
- <span style="font-size: 18px;">**Need training, a professional setup, or a Patrol consultant?**</span> [Contact us directly](https://leancode.co/contact) to discuss how we can support your team.
- <span style="font-size: 18px;">**Want to find out more about our Automated UI testing with Patrol service?**</span> [Visit our service page](https://leancode.co/services/automated-ui-testing) dedicated to automating QA processes in your Flutter app.

<Info>
**Important Note:** Patrol is an open-source project maintained by LeanCode alongside our commercial work, so while we aim to respond and assist as much as possible, our time can be limited. We hope the above-mentioned information will help you find the right channel for your needs.
</Info>
